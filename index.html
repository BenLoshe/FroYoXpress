What is FroYo Xpress?

FroYo Xpress is more than just a frozen yogurt company. Our engineers are working to design systems which increase affordability and accessibility of food, by creating fully autonomous frozen yogurt kiosks.

Our Customers will be able to approach our fully autonomous kiosks, use their preferred payment method, and serve themselves a healthy snack, with amazing flavors that we proudly stand behind.

Our Core Values


Serving Healthy Products

Our great-tasting frozen yogurt is made with the best ingredients. It is dairy-free, all-natural, and the perfect treat for any occasion.


The Self-Service Experience

Our autonomous frozen yogurt kiosk are interactive, simple, and fun to use! Customers simply swipe, pour, and enjoy!


Helping Our Community

FroYo Xpress has committed to donating a percentage of all kiosk sales to The Purdue Center for Cancer Research to support their world-class efforts to end cancer!
